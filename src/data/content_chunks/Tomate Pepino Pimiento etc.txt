EPPO Alert List Purpose of the EPPO Alert List The main purpose of the Alert List is to draw the attention of EPPO member countries to certain pests possibly presenting a risk to them and achieve early warning. It can also be used by EPPO to select candidates which may be submitted to a Pest Risk Analysis (PRA). Pests are marked with an asterisk * in the table below when a PRA is planned or under development within EPPO. The entry date corresponds to the date when the pest was added to the Alert List.
