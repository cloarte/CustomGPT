2022 Spending Plans for the Farmed Cervid Chronic Wasting Disease 
Management and Response Activities 2022 Cooperative Agreements 
 
USDA APHIS VS is awarding $4.8 million through Cooperative Agreements to thirteen entities in twelve 
states. These projects will allow recipients to further develop and implement CWD management, 
response and research activities in farmed cervids, including surveillance and testing. Funded projects 
are listed below.
