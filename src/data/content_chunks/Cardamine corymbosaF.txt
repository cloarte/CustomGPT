Cardamine corymbosaF. - New Zealand Bitter-Cress  -Cruciferae / Brassicaceae(Map updated: March 2008)A recent (post-1985) introduction from New Zealand. Mainly recorded here as a tiny weed in plant pots in garden nurseries, resembling a very small version of hairy bitter-cress (C. hirsuta), but with some plants having no leaves and only a single flower.
