Even if no symptoms of Armillaria root rot have been detected on the trees constituting the natural vegetation, their roots can carry latent necroses from which the fungus can colonize the entire root system after cutting of the tree ( Delatour and Guillaumin, 1995 ). The parasites belonging to the last two categories have inferior pathogenic capacities. These three categories can be found among the Armillaria species.
