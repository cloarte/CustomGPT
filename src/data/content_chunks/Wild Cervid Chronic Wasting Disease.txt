2021 Spending Plans for the Wild Cervid Chronic Wasting Disease 
Management andResponse Activities 2021 Cooperative Agreements 
USDA APHIS WS is awarding $2.535 million through Cooperative Agreements to twenty State Departments 
of wildlife in sixteen states, and one Tribal organization representing twenty Federally recognized Native 
American Tribal governments. These projects will allow recipients to further develop and implement CWD 
management, response, and research activities in wild cervids, including surveillance and testing. Funded 
projects are listed below.
