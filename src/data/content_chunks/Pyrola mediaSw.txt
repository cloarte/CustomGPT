Pyrola mediaSw. - Intermediate Wintergreen  -Pyrolaceae© John Wilde(Map updated: March 2008)A perennial species about 6 ins (15 cm) tall with a rosette of round leaves and a spike of white bell-shaped flowers. Common wintergreen (Pyrola minor) is very similar but the style is short and straight inP. minor, longer and curved inP. media.In heaths, glens and scrub, in all Northern Ireland counties except Armagh. In Co Down it is confined to a few heaths in the very north.All names:Pyrola mediaSw.NBN Atlas mapping:Species account: NBN Atlas UK Species Observations databaseiNaturalist:Species account: iNaturalist World Species Observations database«« Return to previous pageNext Species »
