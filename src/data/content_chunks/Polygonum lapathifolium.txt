It produces 800-850 seeds per plant which, in common with other species of Polygonum , germinate mainly in the spring. A study in Japan ( Watanabe, 1978 ) has shown that seeds of P. lapathifolium have a chilling requirement to break dormancy, with an optimum temperature of 1-5¬∞C. Seed longevity is variable.
