Minor annual brome grasses
Gramineae
Bromus
sp.
Cheatgrass (Chess)
B. secalinus
L.
Downy brome (Bronco grass)
B. tectorum
L.
Japanese chess
B. japonicus
Thunb.
Hairy chess
B. commutatus
Schrad.
Source:
Magness et al. 1971
These are all winter annual grasses that are widely distributed inpasture and range lands and may be troublesome weeds in grain fields. They areprolific seed producers and may become dominant in overgrazed perennialpastures. For a short period in spring they furnish good pasturage. None ofthese is seeded commercially.
