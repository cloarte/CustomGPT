Hypochaeris glabraL. - Smooth Cat's-ear  -Compositae / Asteraceae© Paul Hackney© Alan Hill© Alan Hill(Map updated: March 2008)Plants with yellow dandelion-like flowers are often difficult to identify for the non-specialist. This one is an annual species with a basal rosette of shiny hairless leaves (glabrameans hairless) and bearing the small flower heads on the ends of a branched stem which can reach about twelve inches in very robust plants, but is usually much less.
