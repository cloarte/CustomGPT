Epipactis leptochila(Godfrey) Godfrey - Narrow-lipped Heleborine  -Orchidaceae(Map updated: March 2008)A population of what appears to be this very rare species formerly grew at one site in south-east Co. Antrim alongside common helleborine (Epipactis helleborine). Unfortunately, recent clear-felling of the plantation where it grew may have obliterated it.
