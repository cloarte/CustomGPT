Arbutus unedoL. - Strawberry-tree  -Ericaceae© Graham Day© Graham Day© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)The strawberry tree is one of the botanical specialities of Ireland, but it is not native to Northern Ireland.There are a number of old planted trees growing by the Glen River in Donard Park, near the site of Donard Lodge (demolished), in a wild-looking situation that resembles its native haunts in Kerry. Other planted examples are occasionally encountered in gardens or parks.The tree is remarkable for bearing its strawberry-like fruits at the same time as the flowers, which are white, bell-shaped and typical of the heather family.
