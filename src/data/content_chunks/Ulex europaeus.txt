ous shrub.  The simple, stiff leaves are spinose or acicular, approximately 5-15 mm long.  The typical trifoliolate leaf of the legumes is seen only in the seedling and in plants which grow in rich damp soils.  The plant reproduces by both creeping roots or by seeds.The shiny yellow flowers are solitary or racemose, often clustered at the ends of branches.
