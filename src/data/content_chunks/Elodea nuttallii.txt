Elodea nuttallii(Planchon) H.St.John - Nuttall's Water-weed  -Hydrocharitaceae(Map updated: March 2008)Nuttall's water-weed is similar to the Canadian pondweed (Elodea canadensis) and, like it, is an alien species originating in North America. It grows in the same sorts of places asE. canadensisbut is a much more recent arrival - the first reports in Northern Ireland were in the mid-1980s, and the earliest report in Great Britain was in 1966.It has spread rapidly in the last twenty years, by vegetative means only.
