Ranunculus circinatusSibth. - Fan-leaved Water-crowfoot  -Ranunculaceae© Ian Dodkins(Map updated: March 2008)This species of water-crowfoot produces no floating leaves, and the submerged leaves are finely dissected with their segments arranged into a distinctive fan shape.It is found in mineral-rich still or slow-moving waters and in Northern Ireland is concentrated into three areas: Lough Neagh/upper River Bann, the limestone district of Co.
