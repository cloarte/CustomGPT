Sagittaria sagittifoliaL. - Arrowhead  -Alismataceae© Graham Day© Robert Northridge© Ian Dodkins(Map updated: March 2008)An aquatic plant with leaves of two sorts: submerged leaves are ribbon-shaped; leaves which emerge above water develop the characteristic arrow-head shape.
