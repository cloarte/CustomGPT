Carex dioicaL, - Dioecious Sedge  -Cyperaceae© Ralph Forbes© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)An unusual sedge in that it has the male and female spikes on separate plants. The unbranched flowering stems, which can reach about 10 inches or so, each carry a single club-shaped spike. Frequent in uplant base-rich mires.
