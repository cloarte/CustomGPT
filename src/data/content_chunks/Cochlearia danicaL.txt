Cochlearia danicaL. - Danish Scurvygrass  -Cruciferae / Brassicaceae© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)Danish scurvy-grass is an early-flowering annual species, smaller than the other scurvy-grass species and with a tendency to have pale mauve flowers. The leaves are like small ivy leaves, and the whole plant is much smaller than other scurvy-grass species. It is frequent around the coast on rocks on the upper sea shore; also sometimes on walls and buildings near the sea..All names:Cochlearia danicaL.NBN Atlas mapping:Species account: NBN Atlas UK Species Observations databaseiNaturalist:Species account: iNaturalist World Species Observations database«« Return to previous pageNext Species »
