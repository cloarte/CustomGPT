Hypericum tetrapterumFries - Square-stalked St. John's-wort  -Guttiferae / Hypericaceae / Clusiaceae© John Wilde© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)An erect hairless perennial plant with stems that are square in cross-section and with the four angles winged, reaching two feet in height and with stolons produced from the base.
