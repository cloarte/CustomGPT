Severe infections can lead up to 50% yield loss.  Late season severe infections may look like frost injury.Life Cycle and EpidemiologyThe northern corn leaf blight pathogen overwinters on leaf debris. Spores are blown by wind and can be carried over long distances. Symptoms are more abundant at tasseling and these start in the lower leaves and move to upper leaves mainly from secondary spores produced from initial infection. Severe infections can lead up to 50% yield loss.  Late season severe infections may look like frost injury.Life Cycle and EpidemiologyThe northern corn leaf blight pathogen overwinters on leaf debris. Spores are blown by wind and can be carried over long distances. Symptoms are more abundant at tasseling and these start in the lower leaves and move to upper leaves mainly from secondary spores produced from initial infection.
