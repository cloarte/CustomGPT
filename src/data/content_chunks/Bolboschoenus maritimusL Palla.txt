Bolboschoenus maritimus(L.) Palla - Sea Club-rush  -Cyperaceae© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)A very common species near the seashore in brackish pools, lagoons and saltmarshes; also in estuarine marshes such as those of the Lower Bann. The plant requires high levels of sodium salts in order to grow: in central Europe this requirement can be met in soda lakes and marshes far away from the sea, but in Ireland it is strictly maritime.(Also calledScirpus maritimus).All names:Bolboschoenus maritimus(L.)
