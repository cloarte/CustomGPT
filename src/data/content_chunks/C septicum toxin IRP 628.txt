Precautions: This reagent does not present a hazard to laboratory personnel who work with the 
toxin provided sound fundamental laboratory techniques are followed. 
6. Intended Use: IRP 639 for use in the VERO cell line assay test as described in 
BBPRO1009 for proficiency testing. 
7. Composition of Proficiency Panel: The IRP 639 panel contains 24 vials of the Standard 
Toxin at the working stock dilution of 1:10, 24 vials of the Standard Antitoxin at the working 
stock dilution of 10 AU/mL, and 24 vials of rabbit serum samples. 
8. Instructions for Use: At least one individual at the participating laboratories is required 
to run one entire panel. Testing should be completed as described in CVB-PRO-0009. Copies of 
BBPRO1009 are available upon request and are included with each panel. 
The Standard Antitoxin is diluted to 0.1 AU/mL by adding 1.0 mL of the 10 AU/mL working 
stock dilution to 9.0 mL of DMEM diluent, and then further diluting to 0.1 AU/mL by adding 1.0 
mL of the 1 AU/mL dilution to 9.0 mL of DMEM diluent. 
Rabbit serum samples are diluted 1:10 by adding 300µL to 2.7 mL DMEM diluent.
