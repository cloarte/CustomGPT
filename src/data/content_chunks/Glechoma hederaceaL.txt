Glechoma hederaceaL. - Ground-ivy  -Lamiaceae© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)A  native, trailing and stoloniferous, herbaceous perennial species of the floor of dry woods or dense hedgerows, with stalked, kidney-shaped, toothed leaves, which have a strong, aromatic scent, and clusters of purplish labiate-type flowers at the nodes. Frequent in suitable situations throughout Northern Ireland.All names:Glechoma hederaceaL.;Nepeta glechomaBenth.;Nepeta hederacea(L.)
