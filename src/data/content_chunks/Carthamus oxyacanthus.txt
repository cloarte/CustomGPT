The plant goes through different stages viz., rosette, elongation, branching, flowering, fruiting and drying. The rosette stage lasts for about 25 to 30 days and the plant reaches 50% flowering in 90 to 127 days and maturity in 120 to 165 days after germination.C. oxyacanthusis a short to medium short plant, growing to an average height of 30 to 90 cm. Each branch ends with a capsule. The leaves are sessile, spiny and oblanceolate.  Reproduccion: Genetics Carthamus oxyacanthus is a diploid plant with 24 chromosomes ( Ashri and Knowles, 1960 ). Karyotypic analysis revealed that this species had nine pairs of metacentric (m) and three pairs of submetacentric (sm) chromosomes, and the longest two sm pairs had satellites ( Carapetian and Zarei, 2005b ; Jayaramu and Chatterji, 1986 ). It can naturally hybridize with C. tinctorius if their flowering periods overlap ( McPherson et al., 2004 ; Mayerhofer et al., 2011 ). Close genetic relationship between C. oxyacanthus and C. tinctorius was reported based on cytogenetic, and chloroplast and nuclear DNA studies ( Ashri and Knowles, 1960 ; Bassir, 1977; Pillai et al., 1981 ; Sehegal et al., 2008; Ambreen et al., 2015 ).
