Persicaria lapathifolia(L.) Gray - Pale Persicaria  -Polygonaceae© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)This native species is very similar toPersicaria maculosa(redshank or persicaria) but it has pale green flowers and it lacks the red coloration of the stem. It grows in similar situations toP. maculosa, and often mixed with it. It is rather less common than that species.Polygonum lapathifoliumis an alternative name.All names:Persicaria lapathifolia(L.)
