Ulmus minor ssp. minorSuckow - Small-leaved Elm  -Ulmaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)This elm has the smallest leaves of any of the three species normally encountered within Northern Ireland. It is found as planted trees in hedgerows, mainly in the southern half of the Province. It is less common than eitherUlmus proceraor the nativeU. glabra.
