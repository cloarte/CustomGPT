Lathraea clandestinaL. - Purple Toothwort  -Orobanchaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)This species, introduced from the European continent, is related to our native toothwort (Lathraea squamaria). In Ireland the Purple Toothwort is only known from gardens where it was deliberately introduced. Like the native species it is a parasite of trees, usually willows.
