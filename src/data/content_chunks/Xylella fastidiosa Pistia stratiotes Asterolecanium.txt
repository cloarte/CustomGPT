Regional IPPC Workshop for the Near East and North Africa. Amman (Jordan) 14-17 September 2015
22/10/2015
The regional IPPC workshop for the Near east and North Africa was held in Amman (Jordan) on September 14-17, 2015. The opening was done by His Excellency Minsiter of Agriculture Dr Akef Al Zoubi. Dr Xia Jingyuan, the IPPC Secretary participated also to this workshop.
Regional workshop. September 14-17, 2015
NEPPOPresentation and updates
Draft ISPM. Comments
Draft ISPM: appendix to ISPM 20 on Arrangements for verification of compliance of consignments by the importing country in the exporting country (2005-003) (Appendix XX)
Draft ISPM: revisions to ISPM 15 (Regulation of wood packaging material in international trade) Annex 1 and 2 for inclusion of the phytosanitary treatment Sulphuryl fluoride fumigation of wood packaging material (2006-010A) and the revision of the dielectric heating section in Annex 1 of ISPM 15 (2006-010B)
International Year of Plant HealthPhytosanitary Resources Page and IPPC technical resources
E-phyto update
ISPM 15 Registration: Updated table on situation of countries
Submission of Formal Objections: Process and justification
Dispute settlement in the IPPC
WTO Trade Facilitation Agreement: Update and comments
Review of ISPM 6 implementation in the NENA region
Emerging pests. September 18, 2015
Olive decline disease – Xylella fastidiosa. Mr. Luigi Catalano
Aquatic weed – Pistia stratiotes – Morocco experience. Ms. Rachida El Bouzaidi
Date Palm Green Pit Scale Insect (Asterolecanium phoenicis) – Sudan experience. Mr. Mahdi A. Mohamed
