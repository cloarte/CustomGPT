Trollius europaeusL. - Globe-flower  -Ranunculaceae© Ralph Forbes© Alan Hill© Alan Hill(Map updated: March 2008)This is a handsome plant of lake margins and wet woods looking somewhat like a tall robust buttercup, but with the flowers more globular and compacted.The species is generally distributed across much of northern Europe, becoming more of a mountain plant in its southern areas of occurrence.In Ireland today the globe-flower is very local and is found around about Loughs Melvin and MacNean in counties Fermanagh and Leitrim, and also in Donegal.
