Anthyllis vulneraria ssp. lapponica(N.Hylander) Jalas - kidney vetch  -Leguminosae / Fabaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)This is a more robust subspecies of the common kidney vetch, with the terminal leaflet of the upper leaves much larger than the lower leaflets.
