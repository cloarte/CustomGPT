Oenanthe fistulosaL. - Tubular Water-dropwort  -Umbelliferae / Apiaceae(Map updated: March 2008)A perennial aquatic with hollow stems carrying pinnate leaves with narrow linear leaflets and umbels of white flowers; growing up to about 2½ ft (80 cm). Found in marshes, fens, lakesides, ditches etc., mainly in the Lough Neagh and Erne basins.Somewhat similar in appearance toOenanthe lachenaliiwhich is, however, an exclusively coastal species in Northern Ireland.All names:Oenanthe fistulosaL.NBN Atlas mapping:Species account: NBN Atlas UK Species Observations databaseiNaturalist:Species account: iNaturalist World Species Observations database«« Return to previous pageNext Species »
