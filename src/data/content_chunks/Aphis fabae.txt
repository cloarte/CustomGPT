Three recognized subspecies, in addition to the previously mentioned host plants, attackArctiumandCirsium(Asteraceae),Euonymus(Celastraceae),Viburnum(Caprifoliaceae),Philadelphus(Hydrangeaceae), andTropaeolum (Tropaeolaceae) and others.
