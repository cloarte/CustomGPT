Linaria purpurea(L.) Miller - Purple Toadflax  -Scrophulariaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)This is an herbaceous perennial garden plant which is increasingly seen on roadsides or rubbish dumps, presumably derived from garden throw-outs.
