Bean, Urd
Urud bean, Black gram
Leguminosae
Phaseolus mungo
L.
Source:
Magness et al. 1971
This bean is similar to Mung bean except plants are more prostrate,pods are long and hairy, and seeds are oblong and black. Use is similar to thatof Mung bean.
