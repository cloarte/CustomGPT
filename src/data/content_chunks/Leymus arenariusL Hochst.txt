Leymus arenarius(L.) Hochst. - Lyme-grass  -Gramineae / Poaceae© Fiona McCrory© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)This is a robust blueish-green grass with a thick spiky head of florets. Rare, but increasing, on the upper parts of sandy beaches, associated with the zone of couch-grass (Elytrigia repens). Its increase over the past thirty years may be linked to a warming-up of the climate of the Irish Sea basin.All names:Leymus arenarius(L.)
