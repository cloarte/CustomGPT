Cardamine pratensisL. - Cuckoo-flower  -Cruciferae / Brassicaceae© Graham Day© Fiona McCrory(Map updated: March 2008)Also called the mayflower (the ship on which the Pilgrim Fathers sailed to America was probably named after this species) because of its time of main flowering.
