Botrychium lunaria(L.) Sw. - Moonwort  -Ophioglossaceae© Roy Anderson© Alan Hill© Alan Hill(Map updated: March 2008)© Graham DayThis is a curious-looking little fern with a leaf divided into fan-shaped leaflets and a spike of sporangia arising from its base. The size of the plant varies from about 4 - 20 cm in height.It grows in dry ground in pastures, sand-dunes, on rocky slopes and in heathland. It has a reputation for being inconstant in its appearances from one year to the next.All names:Botrychium lunaria(L.)
