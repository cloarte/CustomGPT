R.Br. - Lesser Twayblade  -Orchidaceae© Robert Thompson© Robert Thompson(Map updated: March 2008)Lesser Twayblade (Listera cordata) is, in many ways, a miniature version of the Common Twayblade. It occurs characteristically in two kinds of place: underneath heather on mountain moorland, or in woods in upland areas where it will be found rooted in moss such asLeucobryum.The plant is quite tiny and is easily overlooked, a fact which contributes to its reputation of being rare.All names:Listera cordata(L.)
