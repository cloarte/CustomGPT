Trifolium arvenseL. - Hare's-foot Clover  -Leguminosae / Fabaceae© Paul Hackney© Graham Day© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)© Paul HackneyA native annual or biennial coastal species of sand dunes,  rocky outcrops, stony embankments; showing evidence of some decline over the past fifty years or so.The plant gets its name from the softly hairy oblong flower heads.
