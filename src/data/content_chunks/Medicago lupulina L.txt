Medicago lupulinaL. - Black Medick  -Leguminosae / Fabaceae© Graham Day© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)A low-growing native annual species with clover-like leaves, each leaflet bearing a minute point at its apex (contrast the similar-lookingTrifolium dubium). The tiny yellow flowers are borne in a rounded head and the black one-seeded pods which succeed them are coiled into a circle - a general characteristic of the genusMedicago.
