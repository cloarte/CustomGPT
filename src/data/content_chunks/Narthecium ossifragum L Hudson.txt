Narthecium ossifragum(L.) Hudson - Bog Asphodel  -Liliaceae© Ralph Forbes© Ralph Forbes(Map updated: March 2008)A beautiful perennial plant of wet and very acidic situations on bogs or moorland, with deep yellow, star-like flowers and iris-like leaves, normally growing up to about eight inches or so in height. Attractive reddish-brown fruits succeed the flowers.Ossifragum means bone-breaker and refers to the brittle bones of animals reared only on the calcium-deficient vegetation of places where this plant grows.All names:Narthecium ossifragum(L.)
