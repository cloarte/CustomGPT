Broccoli Raab
Rapa, Rapini, Taitcat, Italian turnip, Broccoli turnip
Cruciferae
Brassica campestris
L. (Ruvo group)
Source:
Magness et al. 1971
This plant is grown for its tender leaves and flowers shoots which are used 	asgreens or pot herbs. Plants develop rather rapidly and are harvested before theflower buds open. General growth habit and exposure of edible parts are similarto spinach.
Season
, seeding to harvest: About 60 days.
Production in U.S.:
No data, very limited.
Use
: As pot herb.
Part of plant consumed
: Entire top, leaves, stems and flower buds.
