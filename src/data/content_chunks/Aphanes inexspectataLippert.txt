Aphanes inexspectataLippert - Slender Parsley-piert  -Rosaceae(Map updated: March 2008)Very similar toAphanes arvensis. Both are small greenish plants of paths, quarries, hard-packed ground, walls, arable fields etc. The leaves are deeply lobed and the flowers are tiny and green, in clusters which are almost hidden by the leaf stipules. Distinguishing the species requires careful examination of these minute flowers.
