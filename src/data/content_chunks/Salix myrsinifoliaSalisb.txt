Salix myrsinifoliaSalisb. - Dark-leaved Willow  -Salicaceae(Map updated: March 2008)This rather local species was lost sight of for many years, but was re-discovered in the Antrim hills and glens in the 1980s, together with hybrids withSalix cinereasubsp.oleifolia. It has subsequently been identified from a few other areas of Northern Ireland.
