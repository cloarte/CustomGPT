 Sintomas: During spring and summer, the leaves on some branches suddenly wilt and turn reddish-brown. These branches die and become leafless. The wood of affected branches and trunks shows a water-soaked brown or red-brown stain in the sapwood that is often restricted to the outermost annual rings but may sometimes cover the whole transverse section.
