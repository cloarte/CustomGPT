Ranunculus flammulaL. - Lesser Spearwort  -Ranunculaceae© Stan Beesley© Graham Day© Ian Dodkins(Map updated: March 2008)A very common narrow-leaved buttercup of wet situations - wet meadows and pasture, fens, swamps, lake edges, canals and river banks etc. Rather like a small version of the greater spearwort (Ranunculus lingua), which is much rarer.This species is very variable in size and habit.
