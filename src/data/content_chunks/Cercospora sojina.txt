Leaf spots may coalesce to form larger lesions with irregular margins.Symptoms on stems usually appear late in the season under heavy foliar infection. Leaves with large lesions of merging spots may drop prematurely. Infected seeds have poor germination. Even if the infected seed germinates, the resulting seedling suffers from reduced vigor.
