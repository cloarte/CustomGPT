Carex paucifloraLight. - Few-flowered Sedge  -Cyperaceae© Stan Beesley(Map updated: March 2008)This sedge resembles the Flea Sedge, but whereas that species has red fruits, this has pale straw-coloured fruits. Flea sedge is fairly common in marshes, but Few-flowered Sedge is a rare and local plant of upland wet bogs.
