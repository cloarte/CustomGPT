2023 Spending Plans for the Farmed Cervid Chronic Wasting Disease 
Management and Response Activities 2023 Cooperative Agreements 
 
USDA APHIS Veterinary Services (VS) is awarding $5.27 millioni through 20 Cooperative Agreements to 
12 State departments of agriculture and five universities. The funded projects listed below will allow 
recipients to further develop and implement CWD management, response, and research activities in 
farmed cervids, including surveillance and testing.
