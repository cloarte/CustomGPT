Zamiaceae Horianow E. Nasir and Yasin J. Nasir Leaves pinnate; pinnae straight and imbricate in vernation with many ± parallel veins, branched towards the base. Male and female bracts organised into cones, the female scale‑like, peltate, thickened and laterally expanded. Ovules 2‑3. 8 genera and 80 species, tropical and warm temperate Australia, America and Africa. Represented in Pakistan by 1 cultivated species. Lower Taxon Zamia L.
