Remove prior plantings, and control sprouts and seedlings.Bag and dispose of all aerial yams (bulbils) in a dumpster or burn.Treat when new plants are young to prevent seed formation.Pull, cut, and treat when fruit are not present.Burning and cutting treatments have minimal control due to large underground tubers.Recommended control proceduresThoroughly wet all leaves with one of the following herbicides in water with a surfactant before aerial bulbils form: Garlon 3A or Garlon 4 as a 2-percent solution (8 ounces per 3-gallon mix).
