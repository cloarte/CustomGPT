Vaccinium oxycoccosL. - Cranberry  -Ericaceae© Stan Beesley© Alan Hill(Map updated: March 2008)This prostrate, woody plant has tiny oval pointed leaves on long, trailing stems and is rather frequent on bogs. The flower is pink with the petals bent backwards and the pale edible fruit (cranberry) is red or brown-spotted.This is the native European species: it should not be confused with the AmericanVaccinium macrocarpon, a cultivated species which is the source of commercial cranberry sauce and juice.All names:Vaccinium oxycoccosL.;Oxycoccos palustrisPers.;Oxycoccos quadripetalusGilib.;Oxycoccos oxycoccos(L.)
