Eragrostis tef
(Zucc.) Trotter
Poaceae
Teff, annual bunch grass, lovegrass, t'ef, toff, warm-season annual bunch grass
Ethiopian: Tef
Oromigna: Tafi
Tigrigna: Taf
We have information from several sources:
Teff FactSHEET
—contributed by Gilbert F. Stallknecht
Teff: Food Crop for Humans and Animals
—G.F. Stallknecht, K.M. Gilbertson, and J.L. Eckhoff
See:
Teff
—In: The Western Regional Plant Introduction Station: A source of germplasm for new crop development. Bradley, V.L., R.C. Johnson, R.M. Hannan, D.M. Stout, and R.L. Clark. 1993. p. 99-102. In: J. Janick and J.E. Simon (eds.), New Crops. Wiley, New York.
Love grasses
—In: Magness, J.R., G.M. Markle, C.C. Compton. 1971. Food and feed crops of the United States.

Boer lovegrass
,
Eragrostis chloromelas

,
