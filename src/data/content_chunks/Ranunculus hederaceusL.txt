Ranunculus hederaceusL. - Ivy-leaved Crowfoot  -Ranunculaceae© Ralph Forbes© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)A member of the group ofRanunculusspecies called crowfoots, which are white-flowered aquatic species. This species is very common in shallow pools, including pools which are merely temporary; also in drainage ditches and shallow streams, or on wet mud. Unlike other Northern Ireland crowfoots, this has no finely dissected leaves.
