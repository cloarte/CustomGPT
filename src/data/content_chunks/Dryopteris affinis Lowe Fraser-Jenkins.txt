Dryopteris affinis(Lowe) Fraser-Jenkins - Scaly Male Fern  -Dryopteridaceae© Paul Hackney© Fiona McCrory© Fiona McCrory(Map updated: March 2008)© Paul HackneyA complex of several similar plants which differ from the common male fern in having a black spot at the junction of the pinna with the main leaf rachis (central midrib). Some forms eg morph.cambrensisare very scaly - hence the English name.
