Lepidium heterophyllumBenth. - Smith's Pepperwort  -Cruciferae / Brassicaceae© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)Much the commonest of theLepidiumspecies in Northern Ireland. A native perennial or biennial with greyish-green hairy toothed leaves closely set along the often prostrate stems. Flowers in a terminal cluster, small and white. Common on roadsides, in gravels, field margins, railway ballast etc.All names:Lepidium heterophyllumBenth.;Lepidium smithiiHook.;Lepidium pratense(Serres ex Gren. & Godron) Rouy & Fouc.;Lepidium villarsiiGren. & Godron;Lepidium hirtum(L.)
