Aphanes arvensis sens.str.L. - Parsley-piert  -Rosaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)Very similar toAphanes inexspectata. Both are small greenish plants of paths, quarries, hard-packed ground, walls, arable fields etc. The leaves are deeply lobed and the flowers are tiny and green, in clusters which are almost hidden by the leaf stipules. Distinguishing the species requires careful examination of these minute flowers. Both species are frequent.All names:Aphanes arvensis sens.str.L.;Aphanes arvensisL.;Alchemilla arvensis(L.)
