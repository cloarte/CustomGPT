During spring and summer months when conditions become warm and wet, it will colonize outer scales with water-dispersed conidia. It is possible forC. circinansto complete its life cycle in less than a week. Its sexual stage is rarely seen in the wild.Geographic DistributionColletotrichum circinanshas been found ubiquitously anywhere onions are grown.ManagementRotate crops on a 2-3 year basis as a solution for white cultivars.Maintain good drainage.Do not harvest during wet weather.Remove affected bulbs from group when found.Keep stored bulbs dry.Use only healthy transplants.Use colored varieties of onion.Cultivate in soil free of disease for 2-3 years if possible.Consult your local extension specialist for legal and efficacious fungicide products available in your state.
