Hieron - Chilean Hard-fern  -Blechnaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)This introduced species is a large and handsome relative of our native hard fernBlechnum spicant. It was introduced into gardens from South America and persists indefinitely in a semi-wild state at a few suitable sites in gardens and parks, such as Crawfordsburn Glen in Co.
