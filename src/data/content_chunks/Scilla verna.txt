Scilla vernaHudson - Spring Squill  -Liliaceae© Paul Hackney© Paul Hackney© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)© Stan BeesleyA small and individually inconspicuous species of short turf in coastal dry habitats. However, the plants usually grow in large groups, sometimes covering large areas, in which case they colour the area a bright pale blue with their flowers.
