Atriplex glabriusculaEdmondston - Babington's Orache  -Chenopodiaceae(Map updated: March 2008)One of the two commonest seashore oraches (Atriplexspp.) to be found in plenty around Northern Ireland's coast. The other isA. prostrata, and the two are sometimes grouped together for convenience as theA. prostrataaggregate. The fruit is enclosed by two triangular bracteoles, which are fused for some distance above the base, unlike those ofA. prostratawhich are fused only at their bases.
