Molinia caerulea(L.) Moench - Purple Moor-grass  -Gramineae / Poaceae© Ralph Forbes(Map updated: March 2008)A very common and locally abundant tussocky grass of wet heaths, bogs, moorland etc., especially but not exclusively in upland areas.
