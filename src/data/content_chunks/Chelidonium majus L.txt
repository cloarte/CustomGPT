Chelidonium majusL. - Greater Celandine  -Papaveraceae© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)The Greater Celandine is totally unrelated to the Lesser Celandine and is much less common. It is a member of the poppy family although in general appearance it is more like a member of the cabbage family. Like all poppies it produces a latex when the stem is broken. The latex is bright yellow and has been used in folk-medicine.
