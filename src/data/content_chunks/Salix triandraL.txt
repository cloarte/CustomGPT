Salix triandraL. - Almond Willow  -Salicaceae(Map updated: March 2008)Not native in Ireland: an osier willow with many varieties, planted widely in Northern Ireland. Catkins are produced in spring before the leaves, but frequently there is a second flowering in August - September as well.
