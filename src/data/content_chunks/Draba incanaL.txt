Draba incanaL. - Hoary Whitlowgrass  -Cruciferae / Brassicaceae© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)A rare native crucifer found only at three locations: on Chalk near Torr Head in Co Antrim, on basalt on Benevenagh and Magilligan dunes in Co Londonderry.  It is a stiff, hairy biennial up to 12 ins (30 cm) tall with a rosette of toothed leaves around the base and some up the stem.
