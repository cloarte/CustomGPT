Prunus spinosaL. - Blackthorn  -Rosaceae© Paul Hackney© Paul Hackney© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)A densely spiny shrub producing masses of small white flowers before the leaves in spring, and equally large numbers of juicy fruits (technically known as drupes) in the autumn. The fruits are popularly called "sloes" and despite their succulent appearance are far too bitter for human consumption, except as a flavouring in home-made liqueurs.
