Salix pentandraL. - Bay Willow  -Salicaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)Perhaps the most attractive of the willows, with a shiny oval leaf which is fragrant when crushed. The male trees produce catkins with bright yellow stamens in the spring or early summer.
