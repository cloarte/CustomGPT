Juncus bulbosusL. - Bulbous Rush  -Juncaceae© Graham Day© Ian Dodkins© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)© Graham DayA small, very variable, perennial grass-like native plant with stems somewhat swollen at their bases; sometimes the stems flop over and root at the nodes.
