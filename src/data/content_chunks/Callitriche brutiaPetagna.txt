Callitriche brutiaPetagna - Pedunculate Water-starwort  -Callitrichaceae(Map updated: March 2008)A common freshwater species with elliptical floating leaves and linear submerged leaves which have a notch at the apex. It can be confused withCallitriche hamulatabut differs in not having the well developed bicycle spanner apex to the submerged leaves. Like allCallitrichespecies it has tiny inconspicuous green flowers and fruits.All names:Callitriche brutiaPetagna;Callitriche pedunculataDC.;Callitriche intermedia ssp. pedunculata(DC.)
