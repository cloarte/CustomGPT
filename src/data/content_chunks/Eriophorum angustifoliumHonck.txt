Eriophorum angustifoliumHonck. - Common Cottongrass  -Cyperaceae© Stan Beesley© Roy Anderson© Stan Beesley(Map updated: March 2008)Found in abundance on acid peat on raised and blanket bogs. The plant is fairly inconspicuous in flower, but in fruit it produced plumes of white cotton-like hairs which give the plant its common name.
