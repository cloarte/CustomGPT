When a seed germinates in the fall, the developing root system is able to expand over the winter, giving the plant an increased ability to exploit available water and nutrients in the spring.Ecological ThreatBromus tectorumhas the ability to draw down soil moisture and nutrients to very low levels, making it difficult for other species to compete.
