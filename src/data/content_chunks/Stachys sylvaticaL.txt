Stachys sylvaticaL. - Hedge Woundwort  -Lamiaceae© Graham Day(Map updated: March 2008)A common herbaceous creeping perennial of woodland growing up to about 3 ft (90 cm), with a unique and rather unpleasant smell when crushed. The dark purple flowers are borne in long terminal spikes.This species differs fromStachys palustrisin habitat preference, the broader leaves and the characteristic smell, but the two can hybridise where they meet.  Records of this
