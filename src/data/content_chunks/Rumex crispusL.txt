Rumex crispusL. - Curled Dock  -Polygonaceae© Paul Hackney© Paul Hackney© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)A very common dock with the usual type of flower and fruit of this group. The flowers are aggregated into quite dense spikes, and the leaf is rather long in comparison to its breadth, and wavy in outline.
