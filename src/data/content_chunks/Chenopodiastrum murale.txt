 Seccion ambiental: Biology and Ecology Genetics The chromosome number of C. murale is 2 n = 18 ( Bhargava et al., 2007 ). Reproductive Biology The small flowers of C. murale are perfect, possess five exserted stamens, and are likely wind-pollinated ( Blackwell and Powell, 1981 ). No studies on the pollination biology of this species have been conducted. However, other weedy annual species of Chenopodium have been reported to be self-compatible and able to reproduce both by self- and cross-pollination ( Bassett and Crompton, 1978 ). A single plant of C. murale can produce over 24,000 seeds with an average weight of 0.68 mg each ( Herron, 1953 ). The tiny seeds exhibit dormancy and can remain viable for many years.
