Ranunculus trichophyllusChaix - Thread-leaved Water-crowfoot  -Ranunculaceae(Map updated: March 2008)One of a small group of superficially similar aquatic buttercups with white flowers and finely dissected submerged leaves which are known as the water-crowfoots.Unlike most other species,Ranunculus trichophyllusdoes not also produce floating, lobed, leaves; all leaves are finely dissected, even when the plant is growing on mud instead of in water.
