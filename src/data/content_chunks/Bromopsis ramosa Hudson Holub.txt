Bromopsis ramosa(Hudson) Holub. - Hairy Brome  -Gramineae / Poaceae© Graham Day© Paul Hackney© Alan Hill(Map updated: March 2008)A very tall (up to about 6 feet) and stout perennial grass of woods and shady glens, with a graceful panicle of  drooping spikelets, each flower with a long awn (bristle).
