Aconitum napellus agg.L. sensu lato - Monk's-hood  -Ranunculaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)This attractive plant is actually a member of the same family as the buttercups, but the flowers are more highly developed and shaped like the hood of a monk's habit. It is an introduction in Northern Ireland and a popular cottage-garden plant.
