Persicaria amplexicaulis(D.Don) Ronse Decraene - Red Bistort  -Polygonaceae© Paul Hackney(Map updated: March 2008)An attractive herbaceous perennial alien species with deep red flowers, grown as a garden ornamental but with a few colonies outside gardens, presumably derived from discarded garden rubbish containing fragments of the plant.
