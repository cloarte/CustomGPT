Helictotrichon pubescens(Hudson) Piliger - Downy Oat-grass  -Gramineae / Poaceae(Map updated: March 2008)An oat-like native, perennial, grass which grows up to about 2 feet tall.In grassland on base-rich soils, mainly near the coast, on sand dunes etc.This species is somewhat similar to yellow oat-grass,Trisetum flavescens, but has much larger spikelets.
